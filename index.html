About Us

Welcome to Uni Cycle


Uni Cycle is an online store designed specifically for university students. It’s a convenient platform where students can buy and sell products within their own campus community, making it easy to find what you need and sell what you don’t. We’re here to help students save money, make money, and connect with each other.

Why Use Uni Cycle?

Uni Cycle is the perfect place to buy and sell used or new items within your own university community. With no hidden fees and a user-friendly platform, you can easily find great deals on furniture, electronics, and more. Plus, you’ll be contributing to a sustainable future by keeping items in circulation and out of landfills.

03.

Sustainable Shopping

04.

Connect with other students on your campus, creating a more close-knit and supportive community.
